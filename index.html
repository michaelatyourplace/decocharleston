Charleston EDM Scene Tips

Dress Code

Most venues require business casual or cocktail attire after 10pm. Dress to impress!

Book Ahead

VIP tables and bottle service fill up fast on weekends. Reserve in advance for the best experience.

Peak Times

Clubs are busiest Thursday-Saturday nights. Arrive early or expect lines after 11pm.
